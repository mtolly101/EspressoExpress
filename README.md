# Espresso Express

**Pitch:** Fulfill random coffee orders by clicking ingredients in the correct sequence before time runs out. Finish orders to gain points and extra time; mistakes cost a life.

## How to Play
- Click the ingredient buttons to add to your cup.
- Match the order shown on the left, in exact sequence.
- Correct order: +1 score and +5s bonus time.
- Wrong ingredient: lose 1 life and your cup resets.
- Run out of lives or time â†’ Game Over. Click Restart.

**Controls:** Mouse only (left click).
