# Espresso Express

A simple 2D coffee making game built in Unity where you fulfill coffee orders by adding ingredients to your cup in the correct order before the timer runs out.

## How to Play
- Complete as many coffee orders as you can before time runs out
- The current order is shown at the top of the screen
- Click ingredient buttons at the bottom of the screen to add them to your cup:
  - Espresso
  - Water
  - Milk
  - Cocoa
  - Sugar
- The center of the screen shows what is in your cup
- When you’re ready click Serve:
  - If the cup matches the recipe then you gain 1 point to the score and add 5 seconds to the time
  - If it doesn’t match the cup is cleared and there is no score for that attempt and you lose a life
- The timer counts down from 60 seconds and when it reaches 0 the Game Over screen appears
- When the Game Over screen appears click the Restart to play again

## Controls
- Mouse click only

## Features
- Multiple drink recipes
- Random order every round
- Lives system
- Time bonus for correct drinks
- Win achievement
- Clean and responsive UI
- Color flash feedback for correct recipe and wrong recipe
- Simple cup animation
- Button sounds
